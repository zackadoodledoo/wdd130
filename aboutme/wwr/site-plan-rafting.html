Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to show you the thrilling ride of water rafting! You will soon be seeing the world from the adrenaline pumping rapids of the river!

We are here to show you a side of rafting that you may have never experienced before. We are here to show you routes, tips and tricks, gear reviews, and providing informational videos about preparing for your first trip down the river.

Audience

We are looking for anyone looking to go for an adventure. We want people who like nature, great smiles, and a little bit of getting wet and wild. Come take one of the best trips you'll ever experience down the river with us.

Our target audience anyone that has the interest in taking in a whole new experience. We are looking for those that are already have taken a few trips in the past down the river. We are looking for families that are looking for an immersive, fun, memorable trip together.

Our audience will access our sites mainly though the web page. We will make sure our web page is accessible through various avenues like social media, YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, etc. Have all pages linked back to the home web page.

We feel like our audience wants to see inspiring videos. We beieve rafting community appreciates guided out sections of the local rivers carefully surveyed by our experts.

We hope to provide uplifting, inspiring, and up-to-date information.
